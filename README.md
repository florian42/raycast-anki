# Raycast Anki

Review your Anki flashcards directly within Raycast. This extension bridges the gap between your workflow and your learning, allowing for quick, distraction-free study sessions without ever opening the Anki desktop app.

Key Features:

• Quick Review: Access due cards instantly via Raycast's interface.
• AnkiConnect Integration: Seamlessly communicates with your local Anki collection through the AnkiConnect API.
• Workflow Optimized: Keep your momentum by integrating learning into your daily command-bar routine.
Technical Note: Requires the AnkiConnect (https://github.com/FooSoft/AnkiConnect) plugin to be installed and running in your Anki desktop application.
